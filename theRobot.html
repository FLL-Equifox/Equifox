Date - 10/03/2019
This is WALL-e he was constructed as an experiment with box robots after the January competition. Eventually we decided to improve our competition score as a lead up to september; we added an attachment made by our engineer (Mr Brandomeister). It would fall over the cores when it hit them and allow the robot to extract them.

Date - 10/05/2019
Here is an improved version of WALL-e after we scrapped the box robot idea. This one was constructed after watching a livestream from America where they were talking about the City Shaper Challenge and we spotted a lot of pick up tasks, So we decided to make a claw. Again designed by our wonderful engineer.

Date - 18/09/2019
A new design of WALL-e after we realised that on the 2020 Challenge board the robot needs to lift things quite high up. So our engineer construced a Conveyor for the robot to hold. If things go to plan we could be able to do quite a few attachments in one run.
