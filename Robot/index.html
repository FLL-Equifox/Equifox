City Shaper Challenge 2019/2020

Date - 10/03/2019
This is WALL-e he was constructed as an experiment with box robots after the January competition. Eventually we decided to improve our competition score as a lead up to september; we added an attachment made by our engineer. It would fall over the cores when it hit them and allow the robot to extract them.

Date - 10/05/2019
Here is an improved version of WALL-e after we scrapped the box robot idea. This one was constructed after watching a live-stream from America where they were talking about the City Shaper Challenge and we spotted a lot of pick up tasks, So we decided to make a claw. Again designed by our wonderful engineer.

Date - 18/09/2019
A new design of WALL-e after we realized that on the 2020 Challenge board the robot needs to lift things quite high up. So our engineer constructed a Conveyor for the robot to hold. If things go to plan we could be able to do quite a few tasks in one run.

Date - 28/11/2019
We have now added a sweeper to WALL-e so that he can push towers into the correct position It works on the basis of us building the towers and then placing it in place.

Game Changers 2020/2021

Date - 23/03/2020
Getting prepared for 2021, WALL-e is now a pancake and now has a tuned colour sensor, to successfully follow lines. We hope to add attachments as challenges begin. We have also added a few program procedures which should come in handy.
